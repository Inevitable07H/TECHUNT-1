THE PROCESS OF KEEPING THE TRACK OF DATA AND TO ENSURE THAT IT IS SAFE AND ENCRYPTED IS KNOWN AS THE 'TIMESTAMPING'.
IT IS BASICALLY ASSOCIATED WITH THE BLOCK-CHAIN MINING AND THE CRYPTO CURRENCY.
THE TIMESTAMP HAS AN ADVANCED SECURITY ALGORITHM THAT EVEN THE OWNER WILL NOT BE ABLE TO CHANGE ONCE SET.
THE ADMINISTARTIVE FORM OF THE TIMESTAMP INVOLVES UP SETTING THE SERCURE MANUFACTURES INFRA. TO COLLECT THE AND RENEW THE PROCESS OF THE EVERYTIME SETTING OF THE TIMESTAMP.
